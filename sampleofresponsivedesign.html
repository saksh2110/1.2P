What is Responsive Web Design?

Responsive web design is an approach to designing and coding websites in a way that ensures optimal viewing and interaction across various devices and screen sizes.

It involves using flexible layouts, images, and media queries to adapt the content to fit different screen widths, from large desktop monitors to small mobile devices.

Why is Responsive Design Important?

With the increasing variety of devices and screen sizes used to access the web, it's crucial to provide a consistent and user-friendly experience to all users.

Responsive design improves usability, reduces the need for users to zoom or scroll excessively, and enhances the overall accessibility of your website.
